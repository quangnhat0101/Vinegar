- ABOUT US -

Vinegar Food Restaurant was originally a bar in a southern province of Italy which was established in 1985. It was co-founded by Mr. Alessandro and Giovanni who had been working as chefs for more than 20 years. Besides alcohol, the bar was also serving a wide range of Italian and French dishes.

The bar was using fresh ingredients from local farms and with the talent of the two chefs, in a few months the bar become a favourite among the locals. It had also become a famous place for tourists from neighbouring provinces. With the unexpected success of the dishes, in 1986 the owners decided to focus on serving food, and changed the name to Vinegar Food Restaurant. In 1987 the 2nd branch was opened in Rome, and at the end of 1989, more than 30 restaurants were established across Europe.

In 1989, Jadons Ltd., a giant in the F&B industry of Vietnam, saw the potential of the Vinegar Food and had negotiated to buy the restaurant chain. After registering the franchise in 1990, with the finance and management of Jadons Ltd., currently there are more than 1000 branches of Vinegar Food Restaurant around the world.

With the main menu still focusing on Italian and French dishes, the restaurant also added local dishes with respect to the country in which it is located. During the years of operating, the restaurant has received many awards from many countries, and has served many celebrities and important figures. With customer-oriented services, the restaurant continues to grow strong despite economic difficulties.

"Delicious food has to be made with heart" - Mr. Alessandro, Co-founder of Vinegar Food Restaurant
